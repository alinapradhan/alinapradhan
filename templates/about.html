AI-Powered Crop Disease Detection System

Empowering farmers with cutting-edge computer vision technology to protect crops and ensure food security.

About the System

CropGuard AI is an advanced crop disease detection system that leverages state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to identify plant diseases with high accuracy. Our system is designed to help farmers, agricultural researchers, and crop consultants make informed decisions for crop protection and management.

AI Technology

Our CNN model is trained on the comprehensive PlantVillage dataset, containing thousands of images across 38 different plant disease classes. The model achieves high accuracy in identifying various diseases affecting major crops including tomatoes, potatoes, corn, apples, and many others.

Mobile Optimization

The system is optimized for mobile deployment using TensorFlow Lite, enabling real-time predictions directly from smartphone cameras. This allows farmers to get instant disease identification in the field without requiring internet connectivity.

Geolocation & Mapping

Our geolocation module tracks disease occurrences and creates interactive maps showing disease hotspots. This feature provides valuable insights for large-scale agricultural monitoring and enables early intervention strategies to prevent disease spread.
